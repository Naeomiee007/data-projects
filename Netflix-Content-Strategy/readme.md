Netflix Content Strategy Analysis
Analyzed 8,000+ Netflix titles to identify how content strategy has evolved across type, geography, ratings, and genre.

Key findings:

TV shows grew steadily year-over-year, signaling a strategic shift toward retention-driven episodic content
Mature ratings (TV-MA, TV-14) dominate the catalog, reflecting an adult-focused content strategy
The US leads content supply, followed by India and the UK, reflecting Netflix's dual domestic and international investment strategy
Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
Dataset: Netflix Titles (Kaggle)
