# Data Analytics Portfolio

Python-based data analysis projects covering business strategy, 
financial performance, and content analytics.

---

## 🎬 Netflix Content Strategy Analysis
Analyzed 8,000+ Netflix titles to identify how content strategy 
has evolved across type, geography, ratings, and genre.

**Key findings:**
- TV shows grew steadily year-over-year, signaling a strategic 
  shift toward retention-driven episodic content
- Mature ratings (TV-MA, TV-14) dominate the catalog, reflecting 
  an adult-focused content strategy
- The US leads content supply, followed by India and the UK, 
  reflecting Netflix's dual domestic and international investment strategy

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib  
**Dataset:** Netflix Titles (Kaggle)

## 📊 Top 12 German Companies — 5-Year Financial Performance
Built a composite performance scoring model to rank the top 12 
German companies by overall financial health over 5 years.

**Methodology:** Normalized revenue, net income, ROA, ROE, and 
debt-to-equity annually to eliminate size bias, then averaged 
scores for a consistency-weighted ranking.

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib  
**Dataset:** Top 12 German Companies (Kaggle)
