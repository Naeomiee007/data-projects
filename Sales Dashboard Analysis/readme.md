# Sales Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from the Superstore dataset to identify patterns in sales performance, profitability, customer value, and product performance.

The goal is to simulate the type of analysis a business analyst would perform when exploring business data and generating insights to support decision making.

The analysis was conducted using Python in a Jupyter Notebook environment.

---

## Business Questions

This analysis focuses on answering the following key business questions:

1. Which region generates the most sales?
2. Which product categories drive the most revenue?
3. Which categories generate the most profit?
4. How do sales change over time?
5. Who are the top customers by sales?
6. Which product sub categories contribute most to profit?

---

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the Superstore retail dataset from Kaggle.

It contains order level retail transaction data including:

- Order Date  
- Region  
- Category  
- Sub Category  
- Customer Name  
- Sales  
- Profit  

The dataset allows analysis of both revenue performance and profitability across different segments of the business.

## Key Insights

### Regional Sales Performance
The West region generates the highest total sales in the dataset, indicating that it is the strongest performing geographic market.

### Category Revenue Drivers
Technology products generate the highest total revenue, showing that this category is a major driver of the company's sales performance.

### Profit Performance
Technology also produces the strongest overall profit contribution. Furniture generates substantial sales but comparatively weaker profit, suggesting margin pressure.

### Monthly Sales Trends
Sales vary significantly over time, with certain months showing stronger performance. This suggests the presence of seasonal demand patterns.

### Customer Concentration
A relatively small number of customers contribute a large share of total sales, highlighting the importance of high value customer relationships.

### Sub Category Profitability
Some sub categories generate strong sales but relatively weak profit, indicating potential pricing or cost efficiency issues.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

- Analyze the factors behind the strong performance of the West region and evaluate whether similar strategies can be applied to other regions.
- Continue prioritizing high performing product categories such as Technology.
- Investigate profit margins in Furniture to identify opportunities for cost optimization or pricing adjustments.
- Use monthly demand trends to improve inventory planning and forecasting.
- Strengthen retention strategies for high value customers while expanding the overall customer base.
- Review underperforming product sub categories to improve profitability.
