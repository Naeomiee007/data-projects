Top 12 German Companies — 5-Year Financial Performance
Built a composite performance scoring model to rank the top 12 German companies by overall financial health over 5 years.

Methodology: Normalized revenue, net income, ROA, ROE, and debt-to-equity annually to eliminate size bias, then averaged scores for a consistency-weighted ranking.

Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
Dataset: Top 12 German Companies (Kaggle)
